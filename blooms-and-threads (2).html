📖 Our Story
born from love & craft
pg. 04
"Every stitch tells a story. Every bloom holds a memory."
Born in Bhairahawa from a love of natural beauty and the warmth of handmade things, Blooms & Threads began at a small table with yarn, scissors, and fresh flowers from the local bazaar.
Today we craft custom wedding bouquets, crochet gifts, home décor and keepsakes — each piece made entirely by hand, with intention, care, and joy. No two pieces are ever the same.
We believe flowers shouldn't fade and love shouldn't unravel.
— ANUSHKA SHRESTHA founder ✿
